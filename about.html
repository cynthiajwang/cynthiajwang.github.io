Hello again! I’m Cynthia.

My practice is largely shaped by my interests in art, design, and technology. My passion for art began when I first began taking art classes at age six. Since then, I have transitioned from creating physical 2D spaces to digital (also 2D) realms. I hope to pursue a career in product design.

When I’m not learning new software, you can find me searching for web design inspiration on httpster.net, baking bread and genoise, and at the nearest plushie store in town.

Originally from San Diego, CA, I now spend most of my time in Providence, RI pursuing a B.A. in Visual Art at Brown University.
